Company Store Café

Your neighborhood café inside a landmark. Proudly serving Starbucks.

A great café deserves a great space. The Company Store Café offers Starbucks products, breakfast, lunch and dinner on the ground floor of Pottsville's most extraordinary building. Pull up a chair, connect to free Wi-Fi, and take your time. Work from somewhere worth talking about.

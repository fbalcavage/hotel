Why Work With Us

At The Miners 1928 Hotel, we are building something truly special—a hospitality experience rooted in service, professionalism, and unforgettable guest experiences.

We are looking for team members who take pride in their work and want to be part of an exciting new chapter. Whether you are experienced in hotels, events, food and beverage, or customer service—or you simply have a strong work ethic and a great attitude—we would love to meet you.

If you are enthusiastic, dependable, and ready to grow with us, we encourage you to register and attend.

Dining & Drink

Lounge 28

Where the evening begins — and rarely ends on time.

Lounge 28 is not your average hotel bar. Built into an architectural masterpiece, it's a room with twelve-foot tin barrel ceilings, a fireplace, and a custom bar top crafted from the original bank's black walnut and green marble counters. Come for a cocktail. Stay for the atmosphere.
